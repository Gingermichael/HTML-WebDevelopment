What is Rent A Dad?


Our company is a social service platform aimed at giving small children, teens, and young adults an opportunity to have positive father figures in their lives. Many families are not perfect, and life can cause a great deal of people to end up without the guidance of a dad in their life as a result. Some fathers leave families, others are lost to tragedy, a few develop serious health complications, etc. For these family situations Rent A Dad offers a service where people can hire their ideal father figure for brief or extended periods of time. If you or someone you know is in need of a father figure that will help guide them, increase their overall happiness and quality of life, and be the dad they wish they always had, have them contact Rent A Dad today!


How does Rent A Dad work?


Rent a dad offers contract based services where customers can choose a dad currently available on our Hire A Dad page and then customize their contract afterwards. For family placement and integration we offer the following: in home living environments where the dad can live with the family for extended time periods, seperate households where the family can visit and stay with the father, and event based planning where the father attends individual events in the families life. Each contract reserves a specific amount of time (in years) in which the father will be working with the family. Contracts can be renewed, modified, or cancelled at any time with no extra charges. Services are offered at our starting rate of 20/hour spent with the father with compounding discounts for bulk time reservation (up to 60% off!).


Legal Notice!


Our dads are providing rental services only! They have no legal obligation to stay with families after their contract has ended. They are also under no legal obligation to do any activities against their own will. Our contracts are not legally binding family documents such as marriage licenses, adoption documentation, etc. Rent A Dad is not responsible for any illegal activities that our employees engage in.
